Herat University is a public institution of higher education located in the city of Herat, in western Afghanistan. Founded in 1988, the university offers a wide range of undergraduate and graduate programs in various fields, including medicine, engineering, agriculture, and law. With a student body of over 20,000 students, including both male and female students, Herat University is one of the largest and most prestigious universities in the region, committed to providing quality education and promoting social and economic development in Afghanistan. The university has faced significant challenges over the years, including political instability and limited resources, but has remained steadfast in its commitment to educating the next generation of leaders and thinkers in Afghanistan. Herat University also welcomes international students, with opportunities for cross-cultural exchange and collaboration, and is dedicated to promoting global understanding and cooperation. Herat University is ranked as the #5 top university in Afghanistan during 2023. This is the same rank as last year.
